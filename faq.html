General questions

What is decentrex?

Decentrex is a decentralized exchange for Ethereum and tokens. It is open source smart contract powered and blockchain based.

How it works?

The engine of decentrex is Ethereum smart contract hosted inside Ethereum blockhain.
https://decentrex.com website is a cname of https://decentrex.github.io website it is open source, hosted and served from github pages.

Can I interact directly with smart contract to deposit and withdraw coins?

Yes, you can use any of decentrex smart contract functions with decentrex smart contract ABI and preferred Ethereum wallet.

You can find decentrex source code here https://github.com/decentrex/decentrex.github.io

Fee and gas price info

What are decentrex fees?

There is only a taker fee = 0.3%. There are no maker, no deposit and no withdraw fee.

Do I pay gas for performing actions at decentrex?

All decentrex trades are decentralized and recorded in blockchain so you should pay gas to make decentrex smart contract work like with any other Ethereum smart contract.
You should have some $ETH for covering gas price to trade at decentrex.

What is decentrex default gas price?

Decentrex default gas price is set to 40 gwei (may be changed in future). You can adjust gas price using metamask browser plugin.
